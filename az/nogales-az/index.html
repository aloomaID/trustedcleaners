Nogales, AZ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Nogales (Spanish for 'walnut trees'; Spanish: ; English: /nəˈɡɑːlɪs/ or /noʊˈɡɑːleɪs/) is a city in Santa Cruz County, Arizona. The population was 20,837 at the 2010 census and estimated 20,103 in 2019. Nogales forms part of the larger Tucson–Nogales combined statistical area, with a total population of 1,027,683 as of the 2010 Census. The city is the county seat of Santa Cruz County.
